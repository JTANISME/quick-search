Quick Search is a fast Android launcher and search app that unifies results from apps, app shortcuts, contacts, calendar events, files, device settings, app settings, and the web—plus built-in tools like calculator, unit converter, currency converter, and optional AI search.

<b>Search everything</b>
• Apps and shortcuts with smart ranking and typo tolerance
• Contacts with call, text, and messaging integrations
• Calendar events, notes, files, and folders
• Android system settings and Quick Search settings
• 25+ web search engines with customizable shortcuts

<b>Tools from the search bar</b>
• Calculator, unit converter, currency rates, world clock, date math
• Optional AI answers when you add your own API keys (Gemini, OpenAI, Claude, Groq, or compatible APIs)

<b>Customize your experience</b>
• Overlay mode, home screen widgets, Quick Settings tile, or default launcher
• Themes, Material You colors, wallpapers, bottom search bar, and font size
• Import/export settings and granular permission controls

<b>Privacy</b>
Search runs on your device. There are no ads or analytics. Network access is used only for features you enable (web search, optional AI, currency rates).
